Our Mission:

Everyone in the United States at some point or another has been impacted by the inconveniences of First World Problems. Our mission is to help our fellow "first worlders" resolve these problems with ease through your donations to provide the general public with robotic assistants that mitigate these problems.

What is a first world problem?

A first world problem is generally a problem that provides a minor or inconsequential inconvenience.

Examples:

  • Not getting what you want for a holiday

  • Being hungry but not wanting to cook

  • Nothing to drink at home, but you have an unlimited supply of tap water.

  • One pillow is too low for your head, but two pillows is too high.

  • Smartphone is too big for pant pockets.

  • When your charging cable for your electronic device is too short.

  • When you hear your phone ring, but it's on the other side of the room.

  • The portion sizes at the restaurant you are at are too large.

  • Your house is so large that the wifi signal isn't great in every room.

  • When you want to watch something, and there are too many meh selections on Netflix.

  • Your hand being too large to grab the last pringle.

  • The wifi is free, but it's too slow.

  • And many more.
